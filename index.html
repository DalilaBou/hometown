Top three activities to do at Algeria

Enjoy time to vist Casba

Casbah of Algiers is a UNESCO World Heritage site, the Casbah is a historic, labyrinthine quarter with narrow streets, Ottoman-era palaces, and traditional houses. It is the heart of the old city.

Visit Ghardaïa

Ghardaïa in Algeria, known for its unique architecture and location in the M'zab Valley, a UNESCO World Heritage site. It’s a historic city with deep roots in the Ibadi Islamic tradition.

Timimoun the best sun set

Timimoun, is a town in the Sahara Desert of Algeria, often referred to as the "Red Oasis" due to its red clay architecture. Timimoun is known for its stunning oases, palm groves, and traditional desert culture. It is also a popular tourist destination for those interested in the desert environment and Saharan traditions.
